Logistics of Playing

You will have the ability to select a card to play, but the study system (not the robot!) will place it for you automatically. Hovering over a card will illuminate the bin that the card belongs to, as per the rule you were given to teach (this is not the robot's guess of where it goes, just a helpful tool for you).

For example, the one, solid, green, diamond card that is being selected in the image below illuminates Bin 3 even though it is a "One" card because the fact that it is a "Diamond" card takes precedence.
