Great job! The robot learned the rule.

In the actual game, you must decide when to terminate learning, using a button like the one shown below. If you terminate learning but the robot has not learned the rule, you will be asked to try again until you get it correct. Please terminate learning when you think the robot has learned the rule.

In each of the games, if you correctly end the game, you will earn a bonus payment of $1.00. If you end the game too early or too late by 1 card, you will earn a bonus of $0.75. If you end the game too early or too late by 2 cards, you will earn a bonus of $0.25. Any more, and you won't earn a bonus for that round.
